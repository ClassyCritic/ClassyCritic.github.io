Current political projections

Last updated 11/13/15

For the GOP, Donald Trump has maintained his lead. The most recent Rasmussen poll has Trump at 27, Carson at 20, Rubio at 16, and Cruz at 13. Trump has so far avoided any slip in his numbers with a 87.73% chance to win the Republican. Ben Carson currently has a 5.66% chance, and the other nominees all come in at less than 1%.

For the Democrats, Hillary Clinton is still the front-runner by a long shot. Her 98.43% chance of winning the Democratic nomination won't be easy to overcome, especially with Bernie Sanders and Martin O'Malley having lots of ground to cover at 1.19% and 0.00% respectively.
